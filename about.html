About

3DMM EDGE is a subcommunity of the 3dmm community dedicated to making quality movies while also being friendly to newer members that are wanting to work in 3dmm.

3DMM EDGE was founded on September 17th, 2025 by Pvt. Sherman with one purpose: letting people of all walks of life and community work on high quality movies while also taking other newbie 3dmmers under their wing to guide them into making quality movies. 3DMM EDGE currently consists of 17 members and counting, whom work on high quality movies.

The current members of 3DMM EDGE are:

PVT Sherman (Owner), GregoryInc (Co-Owner), musicPlumber (President), MeowArts (Vice President), mouthstitch, Onattien, LT Films, AdeBluBombs, BurgerBenny, Gene, jdawg, McZeeFlix, Cabineet, Shpit, kevinorsomething, Red Scorpion, Articoll
