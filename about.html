About

3DMM EDGE is a group from the 3DMM community, full of talented people, whose goal is to
make and publish high quality 3DMM Movies.

The group founded 3DMM EDGE back on the 17th of September, 2025, for the sole purpose of
letting people of all kinds to release quality 3DMM films online along with helping out newcomers who know little
about the program.
